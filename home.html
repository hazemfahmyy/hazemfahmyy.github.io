Phase I: Foundation

Classical Control & MPC

The journey began with the rigorous physics of motion. I focused on designing Model Predictive Control (MPC) algorithms and mathematical path-planning modules. This provided early autonomous vehicles with precise, deterministic steering and velocity constraints to safely navigate dynamic environments.

Phase II: The Shift

Perception via Vision DNNs

As operational domains grew too complex for rigid equations, the focus naturally shifted to Deep Learning. My R&D pivoted to integrating spatial vision networks. This involves deploying automated inference engines running multi-task YOLOv8 parallel instance segmentation and object tracking feeds.

Phase III: The Present

Functional Safety & XAI

AI is powerful, but it is inherently a fragile 'black-box'. For the last 5 years, my mission has been safety verification. I architect Explainable AI (XAI) diagnostics and automated stress-testing pipelines to guarantee that deep neural networks never fail silently in the physical world.
