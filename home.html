■ Peer-Reviewed Publication Record

Published in ACM Transactions on Software Engineering & Methodology (TOSEM'24)

Safety Analysis of Image-processing DNNs via Clustering-based Approaches

Abstract: This work introduces robust white-box and black-box safety cluster architectures to parse latent execution spaces within deep vision convolutional structures. By implementing dimension reduction alongside unsupervised feature isolators, the framework automatically identifies operational vulnerability pockets, enabling focused retraining blocks that minimize regression risks.

Published in ACM Transactions on Software Engineering & Methodology (TOSEM'23) Presented in Journal-first Track at 38th ACM/IEEE International Conference on Automated Software Engineering

Simulator-based Explanation and Debugging of Hazard-triggering Events in DNN-based Safety-critical Systems

Abstract: Proposes a testing framework linking virtual simulators (Unity/Blender) with explainable intelligence models (XAI). The pipeline generates synthetic variants around failure edges to deduce exact environmental metrics (lighting, occlusion angles) that cause hazard triggers in neural steering networks.

Presented at the 44th ACM International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE'23, USA)

HUDD: A Tool to Debug DNNs for Safety Analysis

Abstract: Demonstrates HUDD (Heatmap-based Unsupervised Deep Debugging), an automated software engineering utility tracking activation maps to identify error root-causes. By clustering uniform activation patterns, the framework isolates systematic model tracking failures without human intervention with 93% verified purity.

Black-Box Safety Analysis and Retraining of DNNs Based on Feature Extraction and Clustering

Abstract: Outlines black-box model analysis strategies where internal weights remain inaccessible. The introduced feature-extraction methodology maps output anomalies back to structural distribution shifts, allowing targeted dataset expansion that increases classification margins up to 34%.

Supporting DNNs Safety Analysis and Retraining Through Heatmap-Based Unsupervised Learning

Abstract: Focuses on structural functional reliability verification schemes for vision models under adverse weather layers.
